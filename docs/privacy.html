华人万年历 - 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年2月

概述

华人万年历（WanNianLi）是一款日历与备忘录应用。我们尊重并保护每一位用户的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

信息收集

本应用收集的信息非常有限：

  • 备忘录数据：您创建的备忘录内容（包括文字和图片）存储在您的设备本地。
  • 账户信息：仅当您主动选择登录时，我们会通过 Google、Apple 或 Facebook 获取您的基本账户信息（邮箱地址），用于数据同步。
  • 设置偏好：您的语言、主题、节假日国家选择等设置存储在设备本地。

我们不会收集以下信息：

  • 位置信息
  • 通讯录
  • 通话记录
  • 设备标识符用于广告追踪

数据存储

您的数据存储在以下位置：

  • 本地存储：备忘录和设置默认存储在您的设备上。
  • 云端存储：如果您选择登录，备忘录数据会通过 Google Firebase 同步到云端，以便在多设备间使用。Firebase 服务器位于安全的数据中心，采用行业标准加密保护。

数据共享

我们不会将您的任何数据出售、出租或分享给第三方。您的数据仅用于为您提供应用服务。

第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

  • Google Firebase：用于用户认证和数据同步（可选）。

这些服务有其各自的隐私政策，建议您查阅。

数据删除

您可以随时：

  • 在应用内删除任何备忘录
  • 退出登录以停止云端同步
  • 卸载应用以删除所有本地数据
  • 联系我们请求删除云端数据

儿童隐私

本应用不针对13岁以下儿童，我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在此页面发布，并更新"最后更新"日期。

WanNianLi (华人万年历) - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

WanNianLi (华人万年历) is a calendar and memo application. We respect and protect the privacy of every user. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information Collection

This app collects very limited information:

  • Memo Data: The memos you create (including text and images) are stored locally on your device.
  • Account Information: Only when you actively choose to sign in, we obtain your basic account information (email address) through Google, Apple, or Facebook for data synchronization.
  • Preferences: Your language, theme, and holiday country selections are stored locally on your device.

We do NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Call logs
  • Device identifiers for ad tracking

Data Storage

Your data is stored in the following locations:

  • Local Storage: Memos and settings are stored on your device by default.
  • Cloud Storage: If you choose to sign in, memo data is synced to the cloud via Google Firebase for multi-device access. Firebase servers are located in secure data centers with industry-standard encryption.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share any of your data with third parties. Your data is used solely to provide you with app services.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For user authentication and data synchronization (optional).

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Data Deletion

You can at any time:

  • Delete any memo within the app
  • Sign out to stop cloud synchronization
  • Uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • Contact us to request deletion of cloud data

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
